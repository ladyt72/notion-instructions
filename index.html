Birthday Party Bookings

  1. Open the Birthday Party Bookings Doc. Contact all bookings from top to bottom and leave a message where there is no answer. - See voicemail script. Include a comment Initials x Date x LM (Left message)
  1. Check availability: Open the Fixi Calendar to view available birthday package times.
  1. Collect customer information: Gather the customer's name, Birthday Child’s name & age, contact details, and preferred date and time for the birthday from the Birthday Party Bookings Doc.
  1. Call customer to confirm: Confirm date and time period suits. Ensure if they want to do Nerf wars, that an appropriate Nerf wars slot is available. Give customer a brief explanation of how a birthday runs - example Script. Answer any questions they may have - see FAQ.
  1. Provide additional information: Inform the customer about any relevant policies, such as cancellation procedures or court rules.
  1. Open Birthday Party Booking Form and go through entire form with customer and ensure all details are entered correctly. Submit.
  1. Create/edit the booking: Click on the placeholder booking and select “Edit only this occurence” and delete the booking slot.
  • Add the new booking to the Fixi Calendar, ensuring all details are correct, always include customer email address.
    • Open the Fixi Calendar and navigate to the correct date
    • Click Actions button - “+New Booking”
    • Input Customer/parent details - Fill in all mandatory fields * & Always Include Email.
    • Select the appropriate court in the "Court" dropdown
    • Select the appropriate event type - Traditional Nerf & Sports Party or Sports Party
    • Set the correct duration for the booking
    • Set the amount of attendees to informed amount
    • Set the remaining fee to total booking fee - deposit paid
    • Double-check all entered information for accuracy
    • Insert comments from slack: Copy and paste recent comments from Referee- in the Party-Bookings channel and paste into fixi comments.
    • Click "Save Booking" to confirm the booking. If Error Occurs - Click dropdown

      If Following Error occurs: this means the customer already exists in fixi, try using the Existing customer function instead to find your customer. Phone # is easiest to search with. If still not found, press OK and insert a new customer profile.

  1. Remove the booking enquiry line: Delete the whole enquiry line in the Birthday Party Bookings Doc.
